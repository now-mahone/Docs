# Kerne Protocol: Visual Guide for Screen Recording

## 1. Preparation Checklist
*   **Browser:** Open `kerne.ai` in a clean browser window (no bookmarks bar).
*   **Data Room:** Open `docs/data-room/README.md` in VS Code or a Markdown viewer.
*   **Financial Model:** Open the "Kerne Financial Model" Google Sheet.
*   **Resolution:** Record at 1080p or 4K for clarity.
*   **Audio:** Use a high-quality microphone for the voiceover.

## 2. Scene-by-Scene Visual Instructions

### Scene 1: The Genesis Thesis (0:00 - 1:30)
*   **Action:** Open `KERNE_GENESIS_NEW.md` in VS Code or a clean Markdown viewer.
*   **Movement:** Slowly scroll through the first three paragraphs.
*   **Focus:** Highlight the terms "vertically-integrated liquidity infrastructure" and "$1B+ valuation."

### Scene 2: Technical Architecture (1:30 - 3:30)
*   **Action:** Open `docs/research/RISK_MITIGATION_SPEC.md`.
*   **Movement:** Scroll through the "4-Layer Stack" section. 
*   **Focus:** Pause on the descriptions of Layer 1 (Vaults) and Layer 2 (YRE). If possible, briefly switch to `src/KerneVault.sol` to show the code.

### Scene 3: The Math & Backtests (3:30 - 5:30)
*   **Action:** Open `docs/reports/APY_BACKTEST_RESULTS_2026_01_19.md`.
*   **Movement:** Scroll to the "Key Results" table.
*   **Focus:** Highlight the "Realized APY 24.68%" and "Sharpe Ratio 33.46" metrics. Pause to let the viewer read the "Max Drawdown" figure.

### Scene 4: The Economics (5:30 - 8:00)
*   **Action:** Switch to the **Kerne Financial Model** Google Sheet.
*   **Movement:** Navigate between the "Revenue Streams" and "TVL Milestones" tabs.
*   **Focus:** Highlight the $1B TVL row and the corresponding "Buy-and-Burn" revenue figures.

### Scene 5: The Data Room Summary (8:00 - End)
*   **Action:** Open the **Institutional Data Room** index (`docs/data-room/README.md`).
*   **Movement:** Slowly scroll from top to bottom.
*   **Focus:** Hover over the links for "Profit Model V1," "Risk Mitigation Deep-Dive," and "Technical Whitepaper." End with the contact information visible.

## 3. Post-Production Tips
*   **Transitions:** Use simple fades between scenes.
*   **Overlays:** Add text callouts for key metrics (e.g., "20.3% Backtested APY", "Sharpe Ratio 33.46").
*   **Music:** Use a professional, low-energy "corporate tech" background track.

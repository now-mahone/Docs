## 1. INTRODUCTION

### 1.1 Overview
This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Kerne Protocol ("Kerne," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information in connection with your use of the Kerne Protocol, including our website, applications, smart contracts, and any other services we provide (collectively, the "Services"). This Policy is incorporated into and governed by our Terms of Service.

### 1.2 Important Notice About Blockchain
**CRITICAL: The Kerne Protocol operates on public blockchains. All transactions, wallet addresses, and associated data are permanently recorded on public, immutable ledgers and are publicly viewable by anyone. We cannot delete, modify, or hide this information. By using the Services, you acknowledge and accept that your blockchain transactions are public and transparent.**

### 1.3 Non-Custodial Nature
The Kerne Protocol is non-custodial. We do not hold, control, or have access to your digital assets, private keys, or wallet credentials. Your wallet interactions are conducted directly between you and the blockchain through your own wallet provider.

### 1.4 Agreement to Policy
By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, you must not use the Services.

#### 2.2.1 Usage Data
When you access the Services, we may automatically collect:

- IP address

#### 2.2.2 Cookies and Similar Technologies
We may use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, local storage, and similar technologies ("Cookies") to:

- Understand how you use the Services
- Improve the Services
- Provide analytics

You can control Cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling Cookies may limit some functionality of the Services.

#### 2.2.3 Analytics
We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Plausible, or similar) to help analyze how users use the Services. These services may collect:
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Geographic information (country, city)
- Device and browser information

### 2.3 Blockchain Data

#### 2.3.1 Transaction Data
All transactions made through the Services are recorded on public blockchains and include:
- Wallet addresses of senders and recipients
- Transaction amounts
- Token types
- Timestamps
- Transaction hashes
- Smart contract interactions
- Gas fees paid

**This data is publicly viewable and cannot be deleted, modified, or hidden by us.**

#### 2.3.2 On-Chain Activity
Your on-chain activity, including but not limited to:
- Deposits and withdrawals
- Token mints and burns
- Governance votes
- Liquidity provision
- Yield positions

Is permanently recorded on public blockchains and is publicly accessible.

### 2.4 Information from Third Parties

#### 2.4.1 Wallet Providers
When you connect your wallet, we may receive information from your wallet provider, such as:

#### 2.4.2 Third-Party Services
We may receive information from third-party services that integrate with the Services, including:

$7.5 Million Bug Bounty Available
Euler maintains one of the largest bug bounty programs in DeFi, with up to $7.5 million available for critical vulnerability discoveries. This substantial bounty demonstrates our commitment to security and incentivizes the world's best security researchers to help safeguard the protocol.

Introduction
Euler's bug bounty program is hosted on Cantina and plays a crucial role in our security strategy by rewarding researchers who identify and responsibly disclose potential vulnerabilities across our modular lending platform.

Program Overview
Our bug bounty program focuses on key components of the Euler ecosystem, including the Euler Vault Kit (EVK), Ethereum Vault Connector (EVC), Euler Price Oracle (EPO), and other essential modules. With rewards up to $7.5 million for critical findings.

Best Practices
When participating in our bug bounty program, we recommend following these general best practices:

Test in a controlled environment to avoid any unintended impact
Document your findings thoroughly
Follow responsible disclosure guidelines
Avoid exploiting vulnerabilities
Maintain professional communication throughout the process

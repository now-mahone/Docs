## PART VII: REGULATORY DEFENSE POSTURE

### Securities Classification Defense

| Factor | Kerne's Position | Evidence |
|--------|------------------|----------|
| Investment of money | Yes (SAFT investors) | Acknowledged |
| Common enterprise | Yes | Acknowledged |
| Expectation of profit | Debatable | Governance token, not dividend |
| Efforts of others | **No** | Decentralized after TGE |

**Key defense:** Achieve "sufficient decentralization" before TGE:
- Multiple independent development teams
- Foundation operates independently
- No single entity controls >20% of tokens (post-vest)
- Protocol functions autonomously
- Governance is live and meaningful

### Stablecoin Regulation Defense

| Factor | Kerne's Position |
|--------|------------------|
| Centralized issuer | No - user-minted |
| Ability to freeze | No - no admin key for freezing |
| Fiat on-ramp | No - crypto-only |

| Payment vs. savings | Savings instrument (yield-bearing) |
